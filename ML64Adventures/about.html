About the M&L 64 Adventures

The Mario & Luigi 64 Adventures is a SM64 Machinima parody fan series of the Mario universe. This fan series is not affiliated with Nintendo or Shigeru Miyamoto. It is a fan fiction in video format.

Join Mario and Luigi as they both go on some crazy adventures in the Mushroom Kingdom. They got friends, family, and enemies.

Main Characters

Mario Pastaman

Status: Alive

Hero of the Mushroom Kingdom, and older brother to Luigi. Figures out solutions on how to defeat enemies. Looks out for his younger bro.

Luigi Pastaman

Status: Alive

The younger brother of Mario, who is absurd and not that bright. Other than that, he looks up to his older bro.


Secondary Characters

Toad

Status: Alive

A mushroom man who works for Peach and resides at here castle. Toad is known to challenge Luigi to video games.

Peach Toadstool

Status: Alive

Ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom who can overdramatic at times. Other than that, she is sweet according to her subjects.

Lakitu

Status: Alive

A camera man and nerd, who is responsible for recording all the footage in the series.
